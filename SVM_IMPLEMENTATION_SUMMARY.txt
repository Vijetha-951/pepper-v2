📊 ALGORITHM DETAILS:

Algorithm: Support Vector Machine (SVM)
Kernel: RBF (Radial Basis Function)
Features: 8 engineered features
Training: Supervised learning with labeled data

RISK FACTORS ANALYZED:
1. is_cod - Payment method (COD = higher risk)
2. past_cancellations - Customer cancellation history
3. order_amount - Order value
4. delivery_days - Expected delivery time
5. order_count - Total customer orders
6. account_age_days - Account duration
7. avg_order_value - Average spending
8. cancellation_rate - % of cancelled orders

RISK CLASSIFICATION:
- LOW RISK: Score 0-40 (green)
- MEDIUM RISK: Score 40-70 (yellow)
- HIGH RISK: Score 70-100 (red)
