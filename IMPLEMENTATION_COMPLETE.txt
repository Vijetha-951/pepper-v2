🎯 HOW IT WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: DATA INPUT
  • Admin clicks "Refresh Analytics" button
  • System fetches all pending orders from database
  • Retrieves customer information and order history

STEP 2: FEATURE EXTRACTION
  The system analyzes 8 key features:
  1. is_cod - Payment method (COD vs Card)
  2. past_cancellations - Customer's cancellation history
  3. order_amount - Total order value
  4. delivery_days - Expected delivery time
  5. order_count - Total orders by customer
  6. account_age_days - How long customer's account exists
  7. avg_order_value - Average order spending
  8. cancellation_rate - % of past orders cancelled

STEP 3: SVM PREDICTION
  • Features are normalized using StandardScaler
  • SVM model processes features using RBF kernel
  • Model calculates probability of cancellation
  • Risk score generated (0-100)

STEP 4: RISK CLASSIFICATION
  • LOW RISK (0-40): Order likely to complete
  • MEDIUM RISK (40-70): Monitor closely
  • HIGH RISK (70-100): Immediate action needed

STEP 5: RECOMMENDATIONS GENERATED
  HIGH RISK → Call customer, offer incentive, expedite delivery
  MEDIUM RISK → Confirm details, track proactively
  LOW RISK → Normal processing

STEP 6: ANALYTICS & VISUALIZATION
  • Risk distribution chart (pie/bar)
  • Trend chart (high-risk orders over time)
  • Summary cards with key metrics
  • Payment method comparison
  • Top 10 highest-risk orders table
  • Daily breakdown statistics
